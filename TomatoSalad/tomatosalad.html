Tomato Salad

Description

This tomato salad is a delicious salad that can be eaten at any time

Now that tomatoes are in season, this is an excellent recipe to try

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Sweet Onion
    • 1 Kirby Cucumber
  • 3 Cherry Sized Mozzarella
  • 1/2 Cup Fresh Basil
  • 2 Tsp Balsamic Vinegar
  • 2 Tsp Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Roughly chop the large onions, sweet onions, kirby cucumbers, and mozzarella
  2. Mix them in a bowl and then add the vinegar and olive oil
  3. Finally, tear the fresh basil by hand and enjoy
