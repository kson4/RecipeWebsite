Steamed Eggs

Here is an example of the final finished product of Korean Steamed Eggs

Description

It is a delicacy that is both quick and cheap to make

Ingredients:

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of Chicken Stock
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 1 Scallion

Steps:

  1. Beat the eggs with a whisk
  2. Optional: run the eggs through a strainer
  3. Mix the eggs with the chicken stock and then add the salt
  4. Put it in a pot, preferably an earthenware pot
  5. Close the lid and let it simmer in medium or medium low heat
  6. While it is cooking, you can prepare the scallions by slicing thinly
  7. Once you can see steam escaping from the pot, check on the eggs and see if they are finished
  8. Once finished, add the chopped scallions as garnish
  9. Enjoy!
